Game rules (click to expand)

Walter White has sent you the ingredients to make his special product. He has provided you some ratios to sum the ingrediants in order to mix the product properly. If you are able to match White's sum you make the product, however if you go over his sum then the process may blowup in your face. Each one of the images below contain a random value. As you click the image that value is added to your mixture. Good luck!
